ENVI-SAFE-TECH

ENVI-SAFE-TECH is an environmental monitoring and rescue coordination platform designed to bridge the gap between incident reporting and practical on-ground response. The solution helps citizens, student volunteers, and response teams collaborate through a shared interface that tracks event severity, location details, response ownership, and progress updates in real time.

Expanded Project Description

Objective

The main objective was to create a reliable platform where communities can quickly report environmental risks such as pollution, unsafe waste zones, flooding, or fire-prone areas, while authorities and support teams can respond with clear priorities and visibility.

How It Works

Users submit geo-tagged incident reports with category and severity. The system validates location details, maps available responders, assigns ownership, and updates progress from reported to resolved. Administrators can monitor multiple zones and share public advisories.

Key Value

The platform improves coordination speed, reduces communication delays, and creates transparent response tracking. It also encourages long-term environmental awareness through preventive tips and localized community engagement.
